# AyeAstra PRD

AyeAstra is an AI market intelligence analyst for business: it continuously watches the outside world, grounds what it finds in the company's own strategy and priorities, and tells each team what changed, why it matters, and what to do next — with evidence for every claim.

| Part | File | Covers |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | [Vision](01-vision.md) | The problem, the two-context architecture, the fusion thesis, positioning |
| 2 | [Strategy & GTM](02-strategy.md) | The market sandwich, launch wedge, ICP, sales motion, expansion path, moats |
| 3 | [Product](03-product.md) | Data model, launch product spec, source strategy, user journey, deferred features |
| 4 | [Pricing & Business Model](04-business.md) | Modular packaging, offers, business assumptions, metrics |
| 5 | [Roadmap, Risks & Decisions](05-execution.md) | Phases 1–4, ranked risks, open decisions, glossary |

Related: [Tech stack](./tech-stack.md)

**How to read this PRD:** Part 1 is the durable vision (changes rarely). Part 2–3 define what we are actually building and selling in the next 12 months. Part 4–5 define how we make money and in what order we build. When in doubt, the narrower interpretation wins — the single most documented failure mode for this product is building the vision instead of the wedge.
