A battlecard is a per-competitor sales asset kept fresh automatically: positioning, strengths and weaknesses, recent moves, and talk tracks — one card per watched competitor.

## Auto-refreshed, human-editable
Sections are generated from your signal archive and business context, and refresh as new signals land. You can edit any section by hand; edited sections are marked with their provenance so you always know what's machine-fresh and what's human-curated, and a changelog records what changed when.

## Where they fit
Battlecards answer the sales-facing question "how do we win against X today?" — grounded in the same evidence chain as everything else. Pair them with your positioning talk tracks from Settings → Context.
