Insights are the fusion layer's output — intelligence that emerges from connecting multiple signals, not from any single change.

## Three kinds of insight
- **Correlations** — several signals moving together ("three pricing signals from PayBridge in two weeks").
- **Deviations** — statistical anomalies against an entity's own baseline: hiring bursts, changelog inflections, unusual cadence shifts.
- **Pattern firings** — a validated pattern's trigger just fired ("companies that do X tend to launch within 60 days"), with a forward look and the pattern's live track record.

## How patterns earn trust
Patterns start as candidates, get backtested against historical data, and are only promoted to validated when their track record holds up. Every prediction is logged and resolved (hit or miss), so an insight citing a pattern always shows how often that pattern has actually been right. Retired patterns stop firing.

## Where insights appear
Insights land in your Feed alongside signals, feed the weekly briefing's analysis, and are searchable through Ask. Like everything else, each insight chains back to the signals and evidence that produced it.
