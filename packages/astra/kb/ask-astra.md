Astra is the AI copilot inside AyeAstra — this assistant. Ask it anything: how a feature works, what a competitor did last month, what your current priorities are, or where to find something in the app.

## Two ways to reach it
The floating Astra button (bottom-right corner) opens a chat panel on any screen. The Ask page (in the dock) is the full-screen version of the same conversation, with your thread history in a sidebar. Threads are shared between both — start in the panel, expand to the page anytime.

## How answers are grounded
Astra answers intelligence questions only from your org's collected evidence — signals and detected changes — and cites each claim so you can click through to the proof. If the archive doesn't have enough evidence to answer reliably, Astra says so instead of guessing. Platform questions are answered from this guide; business questions from your own business context.

## What it can't do
Astra doesn't have your internal data (revenue, churn, roadmap) — AyeAstra watches external intelligence. It also won't speculate about companies you don't watch; ask it to widen coverage instead.

## Tips
Be specific ("What has PayBridge changed on pricing since March?") and use follow-ups — Astra keeps the conversation's context. Thumbs feedback on answers helps tune quality.
