Reports are saved, composable documents built from your existing intelligence — signals, briefing sections, battlecard extracts, insight summaries — arranged as ordered blocks. A report is a curated view of evidence, never new unevidenced prose.

## Building a report
Create a report from the Reports page, give it a title, and compose it from blocks. Because every block references live objects in your archive, a report is always backed by the same evidence chain as everything else in the platform.

## When to use reports
Use reports for anything you'd otherwise assemble by hand in a doc: a competitive summary for a sales kickoff, a "state of the market" for leadership, a diligence pack. The Board pack (see Board Mode) is the formal quarterly variant; reports are the everyday flexible one.

## Sharing and permanence
Reports live in your workspace and are visible to your team. Evidence referenced inside them can be shared externally via public evidence links, which carry their own share tokens.
