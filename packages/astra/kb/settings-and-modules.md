Settings (rightmost dock icon) is where you manage your workspace: business context, modules, billing, team, and learned scoring.

## Settings sections
- **Context** — edit your business context: company profile, positioning, segments, priorities, delivery preferences, and alert routing. Every save creates a new version.
- **Modules** — see and manage which intelligence modules are active.
- **Billing** — plan, payment, and subscription management.
- **Team** — invite members and manage roles.
- **Learned** — review and reset the scoring adjustments learned from your feedback.

## Modules
AyeAstra is one platform with pluggable intelligence lenses. Competitive Watch — monitoring your competitors — is included in every plan. Add-on modules extend coverage to adjacent arenas (for example Product & Market Watch, which tracks market keywords and platform ecosystems you configure). Activating a module switches on its signal categories, briefing sections, and alert types; deactivating gates new output without deleting the history it already produced.
