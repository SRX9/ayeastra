Billing lives under Settings → Billing.

## How plans work
Your subscription covers the base platform (Competitive Watch on your watched entities) plus any add-on modules you activate. Module activation is tied to billing: subscribing to a module switches it on for your org, and cancelling gates its new output while preserving history.

## Managing your subscription
From Settings → Billing you can view your current plan, update payment details, and change or cancel your subscription — changes are handled through the integrated Stripe checkout and take effect per Stripe's proration rules.

## If your subscription lapses
The workspace gates generation (new briefings, alerts) until billing is restored, but your archive is preserved — nothing you've collected is deleted.

## Questions
For invoice or refund questions beyond what the billing page shows, contact your account owner or the AyeAstra team.
