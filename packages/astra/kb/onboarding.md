Onboarding is the guided wizard that sets up your workspace: it builds your business context and starts coverage on your competitors.

## The flow
You enter your company and domain, and the wizard prefills a draft — one-liner, stage, market, positioning, differentiators, segments, priorities — which you review and correct. You pick competitors to watch, set delivery preferences (briefing day, channels, alert routing), and activate. Activation saves business context version 1 and kicks off monitoring: source discovery, first snapshots, and the baseline briefing.

## Resume anytime
The wizard autosaves as a draft — if you leave mid-way, you resume where you stopped. Nothing reaches the scoring engine until you activate.

## After onboarding
Everything the wizard set up is editable later under Settings → Context (same form, versioned saves) and the Entities page (coverage). Expect the Feed to be quiet at first: the engine needs a baseline before it can detect change — the baseline briefing marks the starting line.
