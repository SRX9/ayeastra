Manage who's in your workspace under Settings → Team.

## Membership
Everyone in your organization shares the same intelligence: the same watched entities, Feed, briefings, and archive. Sign-in is handled through your organization's identity (SSO-capable via WorkOS).

## Roles
Members have roles that gate administrative actions — managing the team, billing, and org-level settings are admin capabilities; day-to-day intelligence work (reading the Feed, asking Astra, tracking actions, giving feedback) is open to all members.

## Per-person touches
Actions can be assigned to an owner, missions have owners and members, and Ask/Astra threads are personal to the person who started them — though the intelligence they draw on is shared.
