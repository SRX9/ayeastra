Your business context is the strategy document that makes AyeAstra's intelligence personal: who you are, how you position, which segments you chase, who you compete with, and what you're focused on right now. Every signal is scored through it.

## What it contains
- **Company** — name, domain, one-liner, stage, market.
- **Positioning** — statement, differentiators, pricing posture (premium / value / parity), and sales talk tracks.
- **Segments** — the customer segments you prioritize, ranked.
- **Competitors** — each with tier and your-advantage / their-advantage notes.
- **Priorities** — your current focus items, ranked; signals that touch a priority get boosted and tagged.
- **Concerns** — standing worries the engine keeps an eye on.
- **Delivery** — briefing day, timezone, channels, and alert routing per severity.

## Versioned, never overwritten
Every save creates a new version; nothing is lost. Each signal and briefing stamps the version that scored it, so "why was this rated high in March?" is always answerable — you can see exactly what your context said at the time.

## Keeping it fresh
Edit it under Settings → Context (the same form you completed during onboarding). Update it when strategy shifts — stale priorities mean misranked signals. The engine re-scores from the new version going forward; history keeps its original stamps.
