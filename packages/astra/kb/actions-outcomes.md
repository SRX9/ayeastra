Actions close the loop between intelligence and results. When a signal recommends doing something, one click on "Track action" turns the recommendation into a tracked item with an owner and status.

## The action lifecycle
Actions start open, and are marked done or dropped. Each remembers its source — the signal, insight, briefing, or mission that prompted it — so you can trace any action back to the evidence that justified it.

## Outcomes
When an action completes, you can record its outcome against a KPI ("we repositioned pricing; win rate vs PayBridge +6pts"). Outcomes accumulate into the platform's proof-of-value story: what the intelligence caught, what you did, what it earned. Outcome history feeds mission retrospectives and the quarterly board pack.
