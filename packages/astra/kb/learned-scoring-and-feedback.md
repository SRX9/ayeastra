Your feedback teaches the engine what matters to you. Every "Useful / Not useful / Wrong / Knew it" vote on a signal adjusts a learned multiplier for that company-and-category combination in your org.

## How it works
The adjustments are transparent and simple — no black box. Positive feedback nudges a company/category pairing up; negative feedback nudges it down. Three negative votes in a row on the same pairing triggers a one-tap mute offer. You can review and reset all learned adjustments under Settings → Learned.

## What feedback affects
Learned multipliers influence severity scoring for future signals only — they never rewrite history, and they never override the deterministic parts of scoring (entity tier, priorities, category weights from your business context). Think of feedback as fine-tuning within the frame your context defines.

## Feedback beyond signals
You can also rate briefing sections, battlecard sections, and Astra's answers. All of it feeds quality: it flags what to fix and tunes what to show you first.
