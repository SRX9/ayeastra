Briefings are generated intelligence documents that synthesize a period's signals into an executive-ready narrative. Where the Feed is a stream, a briefing is the story: what moved, what it means, and what to do next.

## Kinds of briefings
- **Weekly briefing** — the flagship. Generated on your chosen briefing day (set in Settings → Context under delivery preferences), it covers the week's material signals per competitor, with analysis and recommended actions.
- **Baseline briefing** — the starting snapshot generated after onboarding: where every watched competitor stands today, so future changes have a reference point.
- **Dossier** — a deep single-entity document.
- **Board pack** — the quarterly executive artifact, viewable in Board Mode.

## How briefings are built
Each section is synthesized only from evidenced signals — a briefing never contains claims that don't trace back to captured changes. Every briefing stamps the business-context version that scored it, so you can always answer "why did we rate this high back then?" Generated briefings pass a quality gate before they're marked ready; delivery (email/Slack) follows your routing preferences.

## Finding them
The Briefings page lists every generated briefing with period and status. Delivered briefings are permanent — they're never edited after the fact.
