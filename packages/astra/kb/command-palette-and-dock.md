AyeAstra's shell works like an operating system: no sidebar, just a dock and a command palette.

## The dock
The dock at the bottom centre holds the main surfaces: Feed, Entities, Briefings, Missions, Ask, and Settings. The dot under an icon marks where you are.

## The command palette (⌘K)
Press ⌘K (or Ctrl+K) anywhere to open the command palette. Type to jump to any page or search across your entities, briefings, missions, and reports — it's the fastest way to move around. Esc closes it.

## The menu bar
The thin bar at the top shows the clock, the watch pipeline's live status, the ⌘K hint, and your user menu.

## Astra button
The floating button in the bottom-right corner opens Astra, the AI assistant, on any screen — see the Ask Astra article.
