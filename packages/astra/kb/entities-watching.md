Entities are the companies, products, and markets AyeAstra watches for you — competitors, partners, vendors, or anything whose moves affect your business. The Entities page lists everything you watch with its role, tier, and signal history.

## What watching means
When you watch an entity, the platform continuously monitors its public footprint: pricing pages, changelogs, blogs, job postings, reviews, and more. Every meaningful change is detected, snapshotted as evidence, and turned into a scored signal in your Feed.

## Roles and tiers
Each watched entity has a role (e.g. competitor) and a tier — primary competitors get the most scrutiny and the strongest scoring weight; secondary and watch-tier entities are monitored with a lighter touch. You set these when adding an entity and can adjust them anytime.

## Entity detail pages
Click any entity to see its profile: what it is, which sources are monitored, its full change timeline, and every signal it has generated for you. This is the place to answer "what has this competitor been up to lately?" in one scroll.

## Adding or removing coverage
Add entities from the Entities page or during onboarding. Archiving an entity stops new signals without deleting history — your archive stays intact for future reference.
